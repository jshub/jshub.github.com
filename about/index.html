About

jsHub is a non-profit sponsoring a number of projects to make the deployment of data capture for web analytics and other online optimization solutions more professional.

The project maintainers are Liam Clancy and Fiann O'Hagan.

Liam and Fiann have many years of professional experience deploying JavaScript for data capture and secure content targeting on some of the highest traffic websites on the web.
